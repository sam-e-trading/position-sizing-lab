What is this?

This is a free educational simulator for position sizing. It uses simplified R-multiple outcomes to show why risk per trade can matter more than being right.

  • Not financial advice. It is a teaching toy, not a trading system.
  • No affiliation implied. It is inspired by classic position-sizing education, including Van Tharp-style lessons, but is an original simulator.
  • No tracking. It runs locally in your browser and stores nothing.
